# MetaPet Schools Teacher Interview Guide

Run this interview after at least one classroom session or at pilot close.

## Setup And Fit

- How long did setup actually take?
- Did the lesson fit a normal class window?
- Did any step create extra admin or confusion?

## Classroom Use

- Which lesson or flow felt strongest?
- Where did students need the most help?
- Did the runtime language feel clear and age-appropriate?

## Safety, Privacy, And Boundaries

- Could you explain the privacy position in plain language?
- Did you feel confident about aliases, local records, and deletion controls?
- Did anything feel too close to a consumer app rather than a school tool?

## Student Response

- What did students seem to understand best?
- What evidence did you actually collect?
- Did any student appear confused, over-engaged, or excluded?

## Recommendation

- Would you run this again in its current form?
- What is the one change that would most improve teacher fit?
- Would you recommend this to another teacher? Why or why not?
