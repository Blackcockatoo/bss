# MetaPet Schools Child Privacy Notice

## What this tool does

`MetaPet Schools` is a short classroom tool that helps your class learn about digital systems, routines, and online safety habits.

## What it keeps on this device

- A class alias chosen by your teacher
- Lesson progress for this device
- Local class notes and totals

## What it does not ask you for

- Your email
- A password
- A public profile
- A social feed

## Who can see it

- Your teacher can use the device during class
- Your school can decide if any class evidence is exported
- Routine classroom use does not need public sharing

## How long it stays

- Local school data is short-term
- Teachers can delete it
- Old local school data expires after the pilot retention window

## Important boundary

This tool is for class learning. It is not therapy, not social media, and not a chat friend that follows you everywhere.
