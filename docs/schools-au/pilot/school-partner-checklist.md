# MetaPet Schools School Partner Checklist

Use this checklist with principals, assistant principals, or pilot leads before confirming a pilot.

## Leadership Review

- Review Pilot Readiness Verdict v2
- Review the overview and alignment document
- Review the staff briefing
- Review the pilot prospectus
- Review `What MetaPet Schools Is / Is Not`
- Review the acceptance runbook

## Delivery Setup

- Nominate one lead teacher
- Confirm the target class and year level
- Confirm the timetable slot
- Confirm the device setup and who owns the device during lessons
- Confirm who will manage local deletion at pilot close

## Governance And Family Communication

- Confirm the ICT/privacy review is complete
- Confirm the family participation process is documented
- Confirm the parent note and privacy notice are ready to send
- Confirm who handles family questions or opt-in records

## Evidence And Closeout

- Confirm the stop/go review date at the end of the first fortnight
- Confirm who owns the pre/post measure
- Confirm who owns the teacher interview and family feedback collection
- Confirm who will keep the incident log
- Confirm who writes the end-of-pilot summary
